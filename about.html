About me / Minusta

Hi, hello and thank you for stopping by my page!

I'm Elena, a 21-year-old photography student from Northern Savonia, Finland. I've been doing photography seriously if not every day then at least weekly for a year now. Otherwise taking photos for fun has been one of my hobbies for many years. I take all kinds of pictures but currently I'm leaning towards product photography/still life and portraits.

If you liked my work or have any questions, head to my contact page and send me a message!
